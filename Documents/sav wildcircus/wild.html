About Us

At the dawn of the 1980s, a troupe of artists founded by Steven Jackson Junior demonstrated his talents on the streets of Los Angeles. Known to the public at the time as "The Phoenix Artists", this troupe will form the embryo of what would become the Wild Circus. This troupe, which gave an eclectic show in which jugglers, dancers, fire-eaters and musicians took part, charmed the public of the streets of the city with its number. These early admirers and artists were far from suspecting that from these beginnings would emerge something magical, exciting and revolutionary that would be reflected on a global scale. Now the Cirque Sauvage is world famous and settles in France on the side of Lyon, Bordeaux and Nice, people come from everywhere to see the impressive shows of acrobats, magicians, clowns, wild animals and many other surprises!
